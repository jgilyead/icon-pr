Gary Easton is a Los Angeles–based publicist specializing in fashion, beauty, wellness, and digital culture. A Las Vegas native and graduate of UCLA’s School of Theater, Film and Television, he has built a reputation for translating cultural influence into long-term brand equity.

At ICON PR, Gary represents a curated roster of high-impact creators and multi-hyphenate talent shaping the next era of digital media. His work focuses on elevating internet-native voices into mainstream cultural conversations, bridging the gap between social influence and traditional media credibility.

Throughout his career, Gary has led campaigns securing placements across leading fashion, lifestyle, and culture publications, while also supporting major honors and cultural milestones for his clients. His work has helped drive recognitions including: Forbes 30 Under 30, TIME100, and USA Today Women of the Year, alongside six New York Times bestselling book campaigns.

Known for a taste-driven, editorial approach, Gary operates at the intersection of culture and strategy—developing narratives that extend beyond virality and position talent for long-term growth. His work often includes cultivating relationships with leading global fashion houses and market-defining brands, translating digital influence into meaningful partnerships and enduring careers.
