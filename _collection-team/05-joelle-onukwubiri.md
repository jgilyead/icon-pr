With over a decade of personal publicity experience, Joelle Onukwubiri has provided consistent results to a wide range of clients — from established and emerging talent to business leaders and CEOs around the world. Joelle has led the PR strategy for numerous product announcements and festivals and helped guide multiple successful awards and TV/film campaigns. She has built strong relationships with business, entertainment, and tech reporters to secure coverage for clients in top-tier publications such as Variety, Hollywood Reporter, Forbes, Glamour, Los Angeles Times, Elle, and GQ, to name a few.

A native of the DC, Maryland, and Virigina area, Joelle has a Bachelor of Arts degree from the University of Maryland, Baltimore County, earning a double major in Media & Communications and American Studies, as well as a minor in History.

Joelle is an avid television and movie lover, constantly keeping up with the latest releases, specifically those from the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
